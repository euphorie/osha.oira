Introduction
============

With a potential audience of some 20 million micro and small organisations throughout the European Union (EU27), OiRA has a key role to play in making Europe a safer and healthier place to work and by doing so, supporting the goals of the European Community Strategy for Health and Safety at Work 2007-2012.

This package contains customisations on top of the standard 
`Euphorie <http://packages.python.org/Euphorie>`_ application
as used for the OSHA-OIRA website.
